About Me

A lifetime of growth.

I grew up in Omaha, NE for most of my life, but moved to Loveland, CO when I was a Junior in High-School. After returning home, I met and proposed to my wonderful Fiance, Qiang (shown above). We plan to get married this upcoming August and we are both excited for this next chapter in our lives.

Some of my favorite hobbies include spending time with my family, watching movies, and spending time outdoors hiking. I remember spending my summers there helping my Grandfather with little projects and enjoying the cool summer air. To this day, every opportunity I get I want to go back down!
